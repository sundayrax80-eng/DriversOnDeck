About Drivers On Deck Recruiting

We help small and mid-size trucking companies fill empty seats without spending all week sorting through the wrong applicants.

Straightforward recruiting help.

Drivers On Deck Recruiting looks at the details that matter: route, equipment, schedule, home time, experience, and what the carrier actually needs.

We help with direct-hire driver placement, pre-screening, interview coordination, onboarding support, and small fleet recruiting.

Built for carriers and drivers.

Carriers get organized candidate details. Drivers get a simple way to be considered for carrier opportunities that fit their experience.

Why I Started This

I created Drivers On Deck Recruiting after working with owner-operator clients who kept running into the same problem: finding dependable drivers was hard, and turnover made it even harder to keep trucks moving.

Through my work helping trucking clients with bookkeeping, paperwork organization, load paperwork processing, invoicing, and other back-office support, I saw how much one empty seat could affect the whole business. This company was built to help close that gap by connecting carriers with driver candidates who better fit the job.

Rooted in real trucking operations.

This is recruiting support from someone who understands the admin side of trucking too: paperwork, invoices, follow-up, and the pressure that comes when a truck is ready but the right driver is not.

Drivers On Deck Recruiting does not find loads, book freight, dispatch freight, or operate as a motor carrier or freight broker.
